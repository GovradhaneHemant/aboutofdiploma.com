Details of different Branches of Diploma

Computer Engineering

Diploma in Computer Engineering provides a strong foundation in computer principles, preparing students for high-demand careers in hardware, software, networking, and data management with competitive salaries.

Electrical Engineering

Diploma in Electrical Engineering is a technical program that prepares students for careers in the electrical industry, including power generation, distribution, and maintenance, and electronics.

Civil Engineering

Diploma in Civil Engineering is a technical program that prepares students for careers in the construction industry, including designing, building, and maintaining infrastructure such as roads and buildings.

mechanical engineering

Diploma in Mechanical Engineering is a technical program that prepares students for careers in manufacturing, automotive, aerospace, and energy industries, designing and maintaining machinery and systems.
